1. Italy

Italy, known for its rich history and stunning architecture, is a dream destination for art and history enthusiasts. Explore the ancient ruins of Rome, savor authentic Italian cuisine, and cruise along the picturesque Amalfi Coast.

2. Japan

Japan offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity. Experience the tranquility of Kyoto's temples, the bustling streets of Tokyo, and the beauty of cherry blossoms in spring.

3. France

France is a country of romance and culture. Visit the Eiffel Tower in Paris, indulge in gourmet cuisine, and explore the charming villages of Provence.

4. Australia

Australia boasts diverse landscapes, from the Great Barrier Reef to the rugged Outback. Dive into the Great Barrier Reef, discover unique wildlife, and explore the vibrant cities of Sydney and Melbourne.

5. Peru

Peru is home to the ancient ruins of Machu Picchu and the cultural richness of Cusco. Trek the Inca Trail, sample Peruvian cuisine, and immerse yourself in the Andean culture.
